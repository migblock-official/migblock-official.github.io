What is Migblock?

Simply put, Migblock is a passion project on-going for over 7 years. Originally a Sandbox Game, after many many rewrites adjustments and improvements as the Team sharpened their skills, the Project was split into 3 sub-projects:

Migcraft, an open source Minecraft Client.
Migblock, a Minecraft-compatible Game.
Migtest, a standalone sandbox Game.
For more information on respective titles, see their pages!

You can meet the Team and community on Discord, we'd love to have you!

You can see our Youtube channel for trailers, tutorials, sneak-peaks, developer stuffs and more!

How to get Started

This is the very beginning of a new phase for the Migblock Project, and as such there's not all that much to do as a player. But there will be shortly!

So please, if you share our interest in this project, we encourage you to come say "Hi!" and join our community on Discord.

We will start play tests at some point, and when that day comes we'd love to have everyone that we can! :D
