Migcraft

Migcraft is an open-source Minecraft Client compatible with offline-mode Servers and LAN Multiplayer.

Our goal with this project is to create a minimalist, performance orientated Game Client under GPL v2.0. We hope to try and capture the "Classic Feel" of early Minecraft and bring it to later versions of the Game.

- Well it's planned to be!

Migblock

Migblock is a Minecraft Compatible Sandbox Game where in which you can do just about anything your heart desires.

With Multiplayer support you can go it alone or bring your friends along. With performance in mind the Game boasts amazing graphics out of the box.

- Or at least that's what we're planning!
